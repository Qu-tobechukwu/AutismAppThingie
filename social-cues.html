💬 Social Cues Page

Here are key social principles with examples. Use the search bar to quickly find the cue you need:

👁 Eye Contact

Try short glances instead of staring. Example: smile at a colleague and glance away.

💬 Conversation Openers

Ask factual questions. Example: “What project are you working on today?”

🚪 Small Talk Exit

Use body language to politely finish. Example: “Excuse me, I need a break.”

📝 Active Listening

Nod or repeat key words. Example: “So you’re saying the meeting is at 3?”

👋 Non-Verbal Cues

Smile, nod, or use gestures to show attention.
