Social Cues

This page helps you understand people’s behavior in different situations. Tap a cue to hear a voice hint.

Friendly Greeting

Principle: Show warmth, but don’t overdo it.
Example: Say hi with a slight smile.

Listening Signals

Principle: Pay attention to body language.
Example: Leaning forward indicates attention.

Eye Contact

Principle: Short, comfortable glances are enough.
Example: Look at speaker, then glance away.

Responding Politely

Principle: Keep responses simple and clear.
Example: 'Thank you, I appreciate that.'

Handling Overwhelm

Principle: Protect your comfort without offending.
Example: 'Excuse me, I need a short break.'
