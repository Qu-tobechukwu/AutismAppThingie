Daily Situations

Principle: Maintain friendly eye contact.
Example: Smile at your neighbor when passing by.
Principle: Use simple greetings.
Example: Say "Hi, how are you?" to a cashier.
Principle: Wait your turn in conversation.
Example: Listen before replying to a colleague.

Professional Settings

Principle: Keep responses concise.
Example: Answer questions in meetings clearly and briefly.
Principle: Ask for clarification politely.
Example: "Could you explain that part again?"
Principle: Observe body language.
Example: Nod to show engagement without interrupting.

Social Events

Principle: Match the energy of the room.
Example: If others are quiet, speak softly.
Principle: Offer compliments thoughtfully.
Example: "I like your jacket, it’s very colorful!"
Principle: Step back if overwhelmed.
Example: Move to a quiet corner to regulate before joining again.
