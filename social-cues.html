Social Cues Guide

Use the search below to find social situations and tips:

Greeting Someone

Principle: Smile and use a polite greeting.

Example: "Hi, how are you?" with a gentle smile.

Starting Small Talk

Principle: Ask open questions and listen actively.

Example: "What hobbies do you enjoy?" and show interest in their answer.

Professional Meetings

Principle: Stay polite, concise, and acknowledge others' points.

Example: "I appreciate that insight. Here’s my perspective…"

Social Gatherings

Principle: Take breaks if overwhelmed, observe others for cues.

Example: Step aside to calm corner if feeling overstimulated.

Online Communication

Principle: Use clear, kind messages; avoid ambiguity.

Example: "Could you clarify this part? Thanks!"
