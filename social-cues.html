Social Cues

Learn key social principles with examples:

  • Eye Contact: Look at someone when speaking, but brief glances are okay. Example: greet colleague, look at eyes for 2 seconds.
  • Small Talk: Ask neutral questions. Example: "Did you see the news today?"
  • Turn-Taking: Wait for the other person to finish before responding.
  • Body Language: Relaxed posture is friendly. Example: keep arms uncrossed.
  • Boundaries: Respect personal space. Example: 1-2 arm lengths.
