About Social Ease 🌸

Social Ease is designed to help people with autism navigate social situations with calm and confidence.

Features include guided challenges, social cues coaching, reflection journals, progress tracking, and a calm corner with gentle animations.

Created with care using a calming pastel palette, accessible fonts, circle-based UI, and voice hints for accessibility.
