This app helps you prepare for social events, practice social cues, manage sensory challenges, calm down, and reflect afterwards.

  • Challenges: Guided actions for different event types (work, parties, public places).
  • Social Cues: Searchable principles with clear examples and short scripts.
  • Calm Corner: Animated calm visual + breathing guidance (only on Calm Corner page).
  • Reflections: Save short notes about how things went.
  • Progress: See how many challenges you completed per event.
  • Settings: Toggle Low-Stim mode, Voice Hints, animations.
