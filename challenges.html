Daily Events

  • Grocery Shopping: Greet one staff member, maintain calm breathing.

Professional Events

  • Networking: Introduce yourself to one new person, ask about their work.
  • Team Lunch: Try one new conversation topic, monitor sensory comfort.
  • Presentation: Make eye contact with one person, speak clearly at your pace.
